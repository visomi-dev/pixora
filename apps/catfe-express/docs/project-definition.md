# 🐱 PROJECT CONTEXT — Catfé Express

## 1. High-Level Concept

**Catfé Express** is a cozy anime-style time management web game inspired by *Penguin Diner*, but set in a warm cat café environment.

Players manage a café run by chibi cats, serving adorable feline customers in a charming, relaxing atmosphere.

The tone is:

* Whimsical
* Light-hearted
* Cozy
* Casual-player friendly
* Visually charming and soft

The emotional goal:

> Create a warm, comforting experience where players feel relaxed while efficiently serving cute cat guests.

---

## 2. Target Platform

* Progressive Web App (PWA)
* Mobile-first (iOS & Android browsers)
* Lightweight and web-optimized assets
* Fast loading and responsive UI
* Designed for small screens first, scalable to desktop

Assets must:

* Be web-friendly (optimized PNG/WebP/SVG)
* Maintain legibility at small sizes
* Avoid excessive detail

---

## 3. Core Gameplay Loop

1. Customers (chibi cats) enter the café
2. They sit at tables
3. They request food or drinks
4. Player serves the correct item
5. Customer reacts (happy/neutral/impatient)
6. Customer leaves tip coins
7. Repeat and scale difficulty

Gameplay Style:

* Time management
* Increasing pace
* Multiple tables
* Combo / tip optimization

---

## 4. Visual Direction

### Art Style

* Cute anime or soft pixel art
* Chibi proportions
* Rounded shapes
* Soft outlines
* Minimal sharp edges

### Color Palette

* Salmon pink
* Mint green
* Latte brown
* Cream
* Soft warm neutrals

Avoid:

* Dark cyberpunk tones
* Hyper realism
* Overly saturated neon
* Harsh shadows

---

## 5. Environment Design

### Café Interior Must Include:

* Wooden floors
* Small round tables
* Cozy chairs
* Shelves with jars or plants
* Hanging warm lights
* Soft decorative plants
* Window with daylight

Atmosphere:

* Warm lighting
* Pastel tones
* Friendly vibe

---

## 6. Characters

### Playable Character

* Chibi café worker cat
* Apron
* Expressive eyes
* Simple animation states:

  * Idle
  * Walk
  * Serve
  * Happy
  * Stressed

### Customers

* Different cat breeds
* Cute personalities
* Small accessories (scarf, bow, glasses)

---

## 7. Food & Props

Food Items:

* Tart
* Sandwich
* Bubble tea
* Coffee with cat latte art

Props:

* Trays
* Tip coins
* Menus
* Tables and chairs
* Order bubbles (no text inside)

---

## 8. UI Style

UI must be:

* Rounded buttons
* Soft shadows
* Light hover animations
* Pastel backgrounds
* Clean vector look
* No realistic textures

Important:

* Leave text areas empty for localization
* Design scalable UI
* Avoid thin lines that break on small screens

---

## 9. Effects & Micro-Feedback

Allowed effects:

* Hearts
* Sparkles
* Soft motion lines
* Bounce animations
* Subtle glow

Avoid:

* Aggressive particle explosions
* Intense screen shake
* Dramatic VFX

---

## 10. Design Constraints

* No hyper-detailed realism
* No dark themes
* No cluttered UI
* Maintain clarity at small sizes
* Keep the cozy cat café fantasy intact

---

## 11. Emotional Experience Goal

The player should feel:

* Relaxed
* Rewarded
* Efficient
* Emotionally attached to the cats
* Motivated to improve tips and speed

This is not a competitive or stressful game —
it is cozy productivity wrapped in charm.
